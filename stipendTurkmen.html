    8. ABŠdaky Yokary Okuw Jaylara Tölemegiñ Ugurlary: Scholarship/Financial Aid
  • Halkara okuwçylara, ABŞyň hökumeti karz berip bilenok. Ýöne, durli-durli görnuşlerde okuwyň tölegini tölap bolar:
  • -“Scholarship”- ABŞda “scholarship” diýip bir usul bar. Turkmen dilinde gowy bir terjime ýok bu söze (kabir kisi “stipendiya” diyip düsünya yöne bu sözuň stipendiyadan başga manysy bar).
  • - “Scholarship” diymek okuwyň töleglerini hökümet, hususy guramalar, hususy kampaniyalar, yada yokary okuw jaýyň üpjün etmekleri. Bu diýmek, sizin okuwynyzyn töleginin kopüsini yada hemmesini tolenip beriler. Her bir scholarship-in öz duzgunleri bar, soň üçin ozüňiz duzgunlerini içgin okap, ýuze tutmaly bolarsyňyz. -Habarlasmak uçin iň gowy yer oz okuwyňyzň “Interantional Students Amissions Office and Financial Aid/Scholarships” atlandyrylan ofis bilen habarlasmaly…
  • -Käbir scholarship bahalaryňyza (atstatdaky ýada tabşyrmaly standart egzaminlardaky) göra berilya
  • -Käbir scholarship bolsa öz gereginize gora berilya ( meselem: ýokary okuw jaýa geçseňiz we eliňizde ýeter pulyňyz ýok bolsa, şony okuwdaky ofisa gorkezip ýüze tutmaly, we soňa gora okuwyň çykdayjylaryny ozleri üpjün edyar). Yenede bir gezek: her scholarship başga dürli-dürli düzgünleri bar. Ozüniz isleýän we tabşyrjak okuwyňyzyň ofisy bilen habarlaşyp öz düzgünleri hakda içgin öwrenip bilersiňiz.
    ​8. Paying for College: Scholarships/Financial Aid
  • For international students, the United States of America is not able to provide loans. However, there are many other ways that you can get help and afford your own education:

Need-based scholarships

  • once accepted to the college, the college analyzes the income and the abilities of the student to afford the tuition.
  • Based on their NEEDS, the school provides the student with the necessary amount to cover his or her tuition.
  • Given by the government, private and nonprofit organizations/companies, and/or the univerity/college itself.
  • There are many types, each has its own rules and requirements, please contact the school’s admissions office for detailed information.

Merit Scholarships

  • a grant or payment made to support a student's education, awarded on the basis of academic or other achievement.
